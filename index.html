2. Who This App Is For

Quick Check App is designed for use by trained maintenance technicians and organizations involved in industrial operations. Access to the Application requires a valid Technician ID issued by EDGE SA. The Application is not directed to individuals under the age of 18.

3. What Data We Collect

3.1 Authentication Data

  • Technician ID: A unique identifier provided by EDGE SA, entered on first launch and stored securely on the device using encrypted storage (expo-secure-store). This is used to authenticate the technician and retrieve their associated profile and permissions.

3.2 Location Data

  • Geographic location (precise GPS): Collected while performing maintenance tasks to record the exact location where each task was carried out. This data is associated with maintenance reports submitted through the Application.
  • Location is only accessed while the app is in use (when-in-use).

3.3 Camera Data

  • QR code scanning: The camera is used exclusively to scan QR codes affixed to industrial equipment for fast identification and data pre-filling in maintenance forms. No images or video are stored or transmitted.

3.4 Audio Playback

The Application uses expo-audio exclusively to play a short beep sound as feedback when a QR code is successfully scanned. No audio is recorded, no microphone access is required, and no audio data is collected or transmitted.

3.5 Maintenance and Operational Data

  • Maintenance checklists and inspection reports completed by the technician.
  • Equipment identifiers and status records.
  • Timestamps and dates of each maintenance activity.
  • Replenishment and calibration records.

3.6 Device and Technical Data

  • Device model, operating system version.
  • Application version.
  • Error logs and crash diagnostics (used to improve app stability).

3.7 Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Direct personal identifiers such as name, email address, or personal contact details of the technician.
  • Payment or financial information.
  • Social media profiles or external account data.
  • Background location (location is only accessed while the app is open).

While we do not collect direct personal identifiers, certain operational data (such as Technician ID and location data) may be associated with an identifiable user within EDGE SA systems.

5. How We Use Your Data

  • To authenticate the technician and provide access to the Application.
  • To create and store maintenance, inspection, and replenishment records.
  • To associate location data with maintenance activities for operational traceability.
  • To synchronize records with Company servers when an internet connection is available.
  • To detect and resolve technical issues and crashes in the Application.
  • To comply with legal and regulatory obligations related to industrial maintenance records.

We do not use your data for advertising, behavioural profiling, or any purpose unrelated to maintenance operations.

Processing of data is based on the legitimate interest of EDGE SA in managing industrial maintenance operations and on the contractual relationship with authorized technicians.

6. Third-Party Services

The Application uses the following third-party service:

Losant IoT Platform

We use Losant (losant.com) as a backend platform for:

  • Technician authentication via webhook (validation of Technician ID and token issuance).
  • Transmission and storage of maintenance report data.
  • Industrial device monitoring and management.

Data transmitted to Losant is subject to Losant's Privacy Policy. EDGE SA maintains a data processing agreement with Losant to ensure appropriate data protection.

No other third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking libraries are used.

7. Local Data Storage

The Application stores data locally on the device using:

  • Encrypted secure storage (expo-secure-store): for the Technician ID and authentication tokens.
  • Local SQLite database (expo-sqlite): for offline storage of maintenance records pending synchronization.

Data stored locally is protected by the device's hardware-level encryption where available.

8. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade Personal Data to third parties.

We may share data only in the following circumstances:

  • With authorized Service Providers: Losant, as described in Section 6, to operate the backend infrastructure of the Application.
  • Within the Company: With EDGE SA personnel who need access to maintenance records for operational and supervisory purposes.
  • Legal requirements: If required by applicable law, court order, or governmental authority.
  • Business transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of Company assets, data may be transferred to the acquiring entity under equivalent privacy protections.

9. Data Retention

  • Authentication tokens: Stored on-device for the duration of the active session. Cleared upon logout or uninstallation.
  • Maintenance records: Retained for as long as required by EDGE SA's operational and legal obligations (typically up to 5 years for industrial maintenance records).
  • Usage logs and diagnostics: Retained for up to 24 months.

Upon termination of a technician's relationship with EDGE SA, account data will be deactivated promptly and deleted within 24 months, unless legal obligations require longer retention.

10. Data Security

We implement reasonable and appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect Personal Data against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. These include:

  • Encrypted local storage for sensitive authentication credentials.
  • HTTPS/TLS for all data transmission between the Application and backend servers.
  • Access controls limiting data access to authorized EDGE SA personnel.

No method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. In the event of a data breach that affects your rights, we will notify affected users as required by applicable law.

We follow industry-standard best practices for mobile application security and data protection.

11. International Data Transfers

Data processed by the Application may be transferred to and stored on servers located outside Argentina. Where such transfers occur, We take reasonable steps to ensure your data receives equivalent protection, including through contractual safeguards with Service Providers.
